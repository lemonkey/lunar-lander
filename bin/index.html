Try to land on the green pads without crashing or breaking the telemetry link by going too high!

1. Click on Flash

2. Hit 'R' to start/reset

3. Hit 'P' to pause

4. Up = Adjust throttle up

5. Down = Adjust throttle down

6. Left = Left booster

7. Right = Right booster

Note: Landing with an absolute velocity > 3.5 in either X and/or Y directions will result in a crash.
